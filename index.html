About

Making the unseen
visible

I'm Gaja Slokar — a multimedia artist currently studying at a fine art academy, working at the strange border where physical reality ends and the digital imagination begins.

My practice spans XR experiences, video games, illustration, and interactive 3D media. I'm drawn to the twisted, the liminal, and the slightly-off — work that feels like it arrived from somewhere adjacent to here.
